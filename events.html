HackTCNJ 2014

HackTCNJ is the biggest tech event of the year. Each year, we bring together over 100 student technologists and professionals to create applications and innovative projects. The result is 24-hour tech extravangaza that culminates in a round of presentations of some of the most amazing things that can be created in a 24-hour period.     Game Nights

    At TCNJ, we study extremely hard and some nights, it's necessary to play harder. This is why ACM hosts Game Nights every semester. Game Nights are events in which students let loose in friendly competition against one another as they play their favorite games. We play board games, PC games, console games, and more.       Outside Adventures

      Not all learning happens in the classroom. ACM offers members the opportunity to step out of campus and explore other places and meet new people. In the Spring 2014 semester, the ACM group visited the Princeton-based Venture Capitalist firm, Tigerlabs. There, our members not only got a tour of the facilities lead by one of the co-founders, but they also got to meet some of the engineers and learn a little bit about what it's like to build a startup. Aside from the Tigerlabs trip, our members also had a strong presence at hackathons and other academic events that took place outside of school. Click on the images to enlarge them.
