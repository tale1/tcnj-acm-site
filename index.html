Association for Computing Machinery

The mission of the TCNJ ACM student chapter is to create a collaborative environment in which all students interested in computing science can come together to share ideas, partake in activities, and learn together. ACM activities include tech talks, group trips, and an annual hackathon, HackTCNJ. ACM also connects students to resources in the computing community such as hackathons, computer science groups, and professional or academic opportunities.
